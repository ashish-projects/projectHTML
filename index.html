About the author
I'm Ashish Raina. I am passionate about prototyping and designing websites & interfaces with focus on UX in order to create something beautiful and enjoyable to use. I have strong experience working with Adobe Creative Suite and also with HTML5, CSS3, Bootrap, foundation, Wordpress. I am a self-motivated person, who is always eager to learn something new to keep pace with new technologies and trends across the designs of UX, UI, Web, Mobile.
